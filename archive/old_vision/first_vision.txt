# Market Shop — First Vision and Architecture Draft (Draft version)

## What the program should do

### 1. Main page:
- Show top 5 products (by popularity)
- Add top 5 products to basket
- Go to other pages (3 pages total)

### 2. Categories page:
- Show available categories:
  - smartphones
  - laptops
  - computers
- Default category: smartphones
- Show products in selected category
- Add products to basket
- Change category
- Go to other pages

### 3. Search page
### 4. Basket page

## Product structure
Each product has:
- Unique ID
- Category
- is_top (for top sales)
- Name
- Price
- Quantity in stock
